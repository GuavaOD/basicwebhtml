How To Make Homemade Pizza?

Ingredients Needed

  • Dough
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Any Veggie/Meat Toppings
  • Olive Oil
  • Oregano

Instructions

  1. Flatten dough
  2. Add marinara sauce
  3. Add veggie/meat toppings
  4. Add mozzarella cheese
  5. Preheat oven at 450 degrees fahrenheit
  6. Place pizza to cook for 12-15 minutes
  7. Let the pizza rest for 5 minutes
  8. Enjoy!!!
