How To Make Homemade Spaghetti?

Ingredients Needed

  • Spaghetti Pasta
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Ground Beef/Ground Porc
  • Parmesan Cheese
    •

Instructions

  1. Boil spaghetti pasta for 10-12 minutes with a pinch of salt
  2. Strain the noodles but keep a cup of the water that was used to boil
  3. Heat up marinara sauce in a pot
  4. Add meat(s) and wait until its fully cooked before skipping to the next step
  5. Add the cooked pasta into the pot and mix well
  6. Ready to serve and top it off with sifficient amounts of parmesan cheese
  7. Enjoy!!!
